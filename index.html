About Me

I am an aspiring developer looking to acquire the skills necessary to succeed in the web/software development field. I attended Indiana University and acquired my B.A. in Psychology. I focused heavily on motivational and exercise psychology during my undergraduate. I acquired my NETA training certification during college and was a personal trainer/wellness coach for a range of clients ranging from the physically and mentally handicapped to competitive bodybuilders and figure competitors. I competed as a heavyweight in OCB and NPC bodybuilding from 2010 to 2014 and reached national level competition.

I worked every available position within a chain of fitness clubs for 4 years, gaining management and sales experience. I also have 8 years of hospitality experience as a server, bartender, and service trainer. Front end design is an attractive area to me because of a history of artistic ability. I taught private violin lessons for thirteen years and have produced electronic and cinematic scores for 15 years. I greatly enjoy learning new skills so I can help those around me succeed as well.
